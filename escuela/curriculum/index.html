THE 7 LEARNING GROUPS OF THE NEW WORLD

  • Group 1 — The Seed Years (Ages 4–6) — Kindergarten–1st equivalent; the Age of Wonder
    • Core Themes: Awareness of self (body/breath), colors & patterns, connection to nature, emotional grounding, curiosity, basic numeracy & literacy
    • Domains:
      • Consciousness Foundations — naming emotions, heart-breath practice, listening to silence, observing without judgment
      • Nature & Earth Play — planting seeds, tending a small bed, textures (soil/leaves/water), “listening walks”
      • Early Math & Patterns — shapes, counting, simple patterns, rhythm in nature
      • Story & Language — simple storytelling, drawing + symbol-making, letters through play
      • Creativity & Play — music, movement/dance, clay/painting/crafting
      • Gentle AI Interaction — AI as storytelling partner, AI to draw their ideas
  • Group 2 — The Sprout Years (Ages 7–9) — 2nd–3rd equivalent; the Age of Exploration
    • Core Themes: understanding relationships, noticing patterns, asking questions, taking care of space and things
    • Domains:
      • Conscious Awareness — breathing with intention, recognizing ego vs feelings, cooperative play
      • Earth & Ecology Basics — soil layers, weather patterns, seasons & moon cycles, 1 “guardian plant”
      • Applied Math — measuring garden beds, geometry through nature, money & simple trade
      • Storytelling & Language Growth — short journaling, mythology from around the world, hero’s journey introduction
      • Creativity & Design — building simple structures, music rhythms, story-based drawing
      • AI Literacy (Level 1) — asking questions, identifying useful vs silly answers, AI as a research buddy
  • Group 3 — The Root Years (Ages 10–12) — 4th–6th equivalent; the Age of Identity
    • Core Themes: Who am I? What do I value? How do systems work?
    • Domains:
      • Consciousness & Identity — ego awareness, growth mindset, emotional vocabulary expansion, intro to shadow work
      • Earth Systems & Life Science — water cycles, photosynthesis, food chains, garden ecosystem creation, herbal medicine introduction
      • Practical Math (Real World) — fractions through recipes, geometry through building, basic budgeting, probability through games
      • Mythology & Human Story — archetypes, tribal stories, symbolic interpretation, story-writing
      • Creativity & Maker Projects — sewing, woodworking basics, clay vessels, rhythm & drumming
      • AI Literacy (Level 2) — fact-checking, prompts with intention, AI for small projects
  • Group 4 — The Trunk Years (Ages 13–14) — 7th–8th equivalent; the Age of Integration
    • Core Themes: Seeing systems, understanding community, becoming responsible
    • Domains:
      • Conscious Human Development — emotional regulation, empathy training, body intuition, longer meditation
      • Earth & Biology in Practice — soil regeneration, compost, permaculture basics, herbal medicine (safe plants)
      • Math for Life — ratios, percentages, algebra through real scenarios, financial literacy
      • Systems of the World — cyclical history, power structures, government forms, media literacy
      • Creativity & Innovation — build something real, performance/storytelling, music composition basics
      • AI in Creation — AI journals, AI-assisted research, early coding logic
  • Group 5 — The Canopy Years (Ages 15–16) — 9th–10th equivalent; the Age of Vision
    • Core Themes: “What is my path?”, leadership & collaboration, seeing the world with clarity
    • Domains:
      • Conscious Leadership & Psychology — group dynamics, conflict mediation, archetypes, decision making
      • Applied Earth & Science — regenerative agriculture, water systems, energy systems, anatomy for health
      • Math of the Real World — algebra (kept), statistics + data, probability, practical finance, cryptoeconomics basics
      • Mythology, Philosophy & Ethics — symbolism, meaning-making, ethical dilemmas
      • Innovation Lab — design thinking, prototypes, community-based solutions
      • AI Collaboration (Advanced) — automation, AI as creative partner, coding fundamentals
  • Group 6 — The Elder Sapling Years (Ages 17–18) — 11th–12th equivalent; the Age of Mastery
    • Core Themes: Purpose, contribution, responsibility, leadership through service
    • Domains:
      • Mastery of Self (Consciousness) — deep meditation, shadow integration, emotional mastery, purpose discovery
      • Earth Mastery — grow a full garden bed, herbal remedies creation, water + soil management, regenerative system design
      • Math for the Future — data basics, real estate math, business finance, light predictive modeling
      • Human Systems & Future Studies — geopolitics, civilizational cycles, decentralized governance, collapse & renewal
      • Capstone Innovation Project — build a product/system/movement/garden; a community solution; an AI tool that supports it
      • AI Mastery — automation, creative production, research, ethical understanding, personal AI assistant creation
  • Group 7 — The Emerging Elders (Post-18) — The Apprenticeship Path
    • Path: Some return to teach. Some become healers. Some become cultivators. Some become leaders. Some become innovators.
    • Purpose: They learn to serve and anchor the next generation.
