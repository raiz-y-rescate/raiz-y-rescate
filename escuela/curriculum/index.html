What changes

Instead of separating life into Math / English / Science / History… we teach by domains that match reality, and we measure growth by skills, character, and contribution.

This page is intentionally “bones.” Next we’ll add printable templates, lesson rhythms, and a simple navigation for teachers.

Core Domains (replace traditional subjects)

  • Communication — reading, writing, speaking, languages, storytelling
  • Logic & Systems — math as pattern, reasoning, models, cause/effect
  • Nature & Life — ecology, health, food, energy, climate, observation
  • Human Story — history as cycles, civics, ethics, media literacy
  • Making & Building — hands-on creation, tools, design, repair
  • Digital & AI Literacy — using tools wisely, prompts, verification
  • Self & Relationship — emotional skills, boundaries, community, service
  • Economics & Stewardship — value, trade, budgets, resources, land

Assessment (simple and human)

  • Skills: can the learner do it?
  • Understanding: can they explain it simply?
  • Character: responsibility, courage, kindness, resilience
  • Contribution: did this help the home/community?

We can later add rubrics that teachers can print in one page.

Levels (Pre-K → Senior)

  • Level 0: Pre-K (Ages ~3–5) — Body, senses, play, rhythm, belonging, nature connection
  • Level 1: Foundations (K–2) — Language basics, number sense, observation, kindness, simple making
  • Level 2: Builders (3–5) — Reading to learn, projects, maps/timelines, gardens, simple coding, community roles
  • Level 3: Navigators (6–8) — Systems thinking, media literacy, health, practical finance, deeper ecology, teamwork
  • Level 4: Apprentices (9–10) — Skill tracks (making/building, digital/AI, land/food, communication), internships, portfolios
  • Level 5: Pathmakers (11–12) — Capstone projects, community contribution, entrepreneurship/service, civic understanding, life readiness

Monthly Rhythm (teacher-friendly)

  • Week 1: Choose a theme (Water / Food / Shelter / Story / Health / Energy)
  • Week 2: Learn + practice (2 domains)
  • Week 3: Build something real (project)
  • Week 4: Share + reflect (portfolio + contribution)
