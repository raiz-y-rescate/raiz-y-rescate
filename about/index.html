About Sobre

Why this space exists, where it comes from, and the perspective guiding its direction. Por qué existe este espacio, de dónde nace y la perspectiva que guía su dirección.

Raíz y Rescate is a portal for rebuilding: the inner world (emotions), the outer world (skills), and the living world (land). This is not academic education — it is life-centered learning. Raíz y Rescate es un portal para reconstruir: el mundo interior (emociones), el mundo exterior (habilidades) y el mundo vivo (la tierra). Esto no es educación académica — es aprendizaje centrado en la vida.

Why this exists

I grew up within tradition, faith, and structure. Over time, curiosity led me into history, patterns, and deeper questions; not to reject belief, but to understand it more fully.

What changed

Learning how civilizations rise and fall, how education shapes identity, and how disconnection from land affects health shifted how I see the world; not toward fear, but toward responsibility.

What this is (and isn’t)

Raíz y Rescate is not a prediction, a movement, or a doctrine. It does not aim to persuade or warn. It exists as a collection of ideas, practices, and tools for those who feel called to rebuild, calmly, locally, and with care.

Your role

I am not here to lead anyone. I share what I am learning so teachers, builders, parents, and communities can adapt these ideas where they live, in ways that make sense for their land, culture, and needs.

Principles Principios

  • Circle over classroom: learning happens in relationship. Círculo antes que aula: el aprendizaje ocurre en relación.
  • Land as teacher: nature trains attention, humility, and resilience. La tierra como maestra: la naturaleza entrena la atención, la humildad y la resiliencia.
  • Skill is dignity: build, grow, repair, cook, create. La habilidad es dignidad: construir, cultivar, reparar, cocinar, crear.
  • AI as support: a quiet co-pilot — never replacing humans. La IA como apoyo: un copiloto silencioso — nunca un reemplazo humano.
