About

Raíz y Rescate is a portal for rebuilding: the inner world (emotions), the outer world (skills), and the living world (land). This is not academic education — it is life-centered learning.

Principles

  • Circle over classroom: learning happens in relationship.
  • Land as teacher: nature trains attention, humility, and resilience.
  • Skill is dignity: build, grow, repair, cook, create.
  • AI as support: a quiet co-pilot, not a replacement for humans.

How to use this site

  1. Start in Learning.
  2. Explore Land and Structures.
  3. Build your own local circle and adapt what you find here.
